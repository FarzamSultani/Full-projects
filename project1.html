Groot is a fictional character from Marvel Comics, a sentient tree-like creature that can only say “I am Groot” 1. He is a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy and has appeared in various comics, films, and media. Groot’s first appearance was in Tales to Astonish #13 in November 1960 1. He was created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, and Jack Kirby. Groot is a Flora colossus from Planet X, the capital of the branch worlds 1. He has superhuman strength, stamina, endurance, durability, and senses.
